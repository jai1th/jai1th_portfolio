$ cat problem.md

No cloud, no operator, no daylight

Feral animals do damage at night, in places without connectivity. The system had to see in the dark, decide on-device, and act — within the compute and power budget of an embedded board.

$ ./approach.sh
01Deployed YOLOv8 on a Jetson Nano with an IMX219 night-vision camera — optimized to a sustained 24 FPS at 720p.
02Wired detections to trigger non-lethal deterrence autonomously — a full sense-decide-act loop.
03Ran the project end-to-end over two years: proposal → competitive research grant → build → field deployment.
